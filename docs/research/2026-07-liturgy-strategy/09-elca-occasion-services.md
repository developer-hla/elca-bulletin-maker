# ELCA / ELW Occasion-Service Ordos, Text Provenance & Access

July 20, 2026 · Sourcing research for the ELCA liturgy library (Service of the Word, Funeral,
Marriage, Baptism, Daily Office, Ash Wednesday/Triduum). Feeds the "author ordos → owner
verifies → texts via entitlement" pipeline.

## Key structural facts
- **ELW (2006) consolidated everything into the one ~1,211-page pew book** — marriage, funeral,
  baptism, the three Daily Prayer offices (Morning/Evening/Night), confession, and proper
  services for Ash Wednesday/Palm Sunday/Triduum are all bound in, unlike the LBW-1978 era's
  separate *Occasional Services* volume. One authoritative source.
  [ELW – Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evangelical_Lutheran_Worship)
- **S&S covers occasion services**, not just Sunday: its own marketing lists "Weddings, Funerals,
  Morning or Evening Prayer, Midweek Lenten services, and more," under the same Standard/Deluxe
  tiers ($249–799/yr) + the **Augsburg Fortress Liturgies License** (bundled, subscription-
  contingent) that permits reproducing AF liturgy text/music in a church's bulletin. **This
  answers our open question: occasion-service AF texts resolve through the church's existing S&S
  entitlement, exactly like Sunday content — no new licensing lane.**
- The AF license does NOT cover: other publishers' hymns (OneLicense/CCLI), or non-AF scripture
  translations (each Bible publisher's own regime). Note **Holden Evening Prayer** (Haugen) is
  GIA-copyrighted, a separate lane from AF; the Exsultet's modern translation may be separately
  licensed.

## Text-provenance legend
**[PD]** bundle literal text now (ELLC/ICET common texts + scripture-derived): creeds, Lord's
Prayer, Gloria Patri, Kyrie/Gloria/Sanctus/Agnus Dei, Phos Hilaron, the gospel canticles
(Benedictus/Magnificat/Nunc Dimittis), psalms (in a PD/ELLC translation). · **[LIC]** AF/ELW-
copyrighted proper/prayer/rubric wording — gate on the church's AF Liturgies License (same as
Sunday). · **[VAR]** always per-occasion: readings, hymns, names, dates, chosen prayers, petitions.

## Ordos (DRAFT — verify against ELW)

### Service of the Word (Holy Communion minus the Meal)
Confession & Forgiveness (optional) → Gathering song → Greeting[LIC] → Kyrie[PD] (opt) →
Hymn of Praise/Gloria[PD] (omit Advent/Lent) → Prayer of the Day[LIC/VAR] → First Reading[VAR] →
Psalm[VAR] → Second Reading[VAR] → Gospel Acclamation → Gospel[VAR] → Sermon[VAR] →
Hymn of the Day[VAR] → Creed[PD] → Prayers of Intercession[VAR+LIC framework] → Peace[LIC] →
**Offering (retained without the Meal)** → **Lord's Prayer[PD] (moved up, no Great Thanksgiving)**
→ Blessing[LIC] → Sending Song[VAR] → Dismissal[LIC]. *No schema extension needed; close to the
existing Holy Communion rite with the meal removed and the Lord's Prayer relocated.*

### Funeral / Burial of the Dead  (model as TWO linked rites: Funeral + Committal)
**Funeral:** Greeting/anthem[LIC, "I am the resurrection…"] → Thanksgiving for Baptism (pall)[LIC,
VAR name] → Procession/Hymn → First Reading/Psalm/Second Reading/Gospel[VAR] → Sermon[VAR] →
Creed[PD] (opt) → Prayers[VAR+LIC] → [optional full Meal] → Commendation[LIC, VAR name] → Sending.
**Committal (separate, graveside — its own bulletin/insert):** Scripture sentences[VAR/PD] →
Words of Committal[LIC, "earth to earth, ashes to ashes…", VAR] → Lord's Prayer[PD] → Prayers[LIC+VAR]
→ Blessing[LIC]. *Needs the linked/child-rite capability (Committal is structurally distinct).*
Varies: name/dates, family-chosen readings (often off-lectionary), whether Communion is included,
whether committal is same-day or separate.

### Marriage
Gathering/Greeting → Hymn(opt) → Readings[VAR] → Homily(opt)[VAR] → Declaration of Intention[LIC,
VAR names] → Vows[LIC framework, VAR names — the most copyright-sensitive, most-reproduced block]
→ Ring exchange/blessing[LIC] → Pronouncement[LIC] → Prayers incl. Lord's Prayer[PD] →
Blessing of the Marriage[LIC] → Peace(opt) → [optional Meal] → Sending/Blessing/Dismissal[LIC].
*Single rite, no schema extension; heavily [LIC].*

### Holy Baptism (verify the existing module matches)
Presentation[VAR names/sponsors] → Renunciation questions[LIC] → Apostles' Creed interrogative[PD]
→ Thanksgiving at the Font[LIC, flood prayer] → Baptism (Trinitarian formula)[LIC framing, VAR name]
→ (opt) garment/candle → Welcome & sealing[LIC, VAR name] → Congregational welcome[LIC].

### Daily Office — shared shape: Opening → Psalmody → Reading → Gospel Canticle → Prayers → Blessing
- **Morning Prayer (Matins):** Opening versicles[PD] → Psalmody → Reading+responsory → **Benedictus**
  (Luke 1:68-79)[PD] → Hymn → Prayers (Kyrie/Lord's Prayer/collect)[PD+LIC] → Blessing.
- **Evening Prayer (Vespers):** Service of Light + **Phos Hilaron**[PD text/LIC framing] → Psalmody
  (Ps 141) → Reading+responsory → **Magnificat** (Luke 1:46-55)[PD] → Prayers → Blessing.
- **Night Prayer (Compline):** brief Confession → Psalmody (Ps 91/134) → short reading →
  responsory ("Into your hands…") → **Nunc Dimittis** (Luke 2:29-32)[PD] → Prayers/collect → Blessing.
- Mostly [PD] (canticles/psalms/scripture) with [LIC] framing prayers/versicles. Translation
  choice matters (ELLC canticle translations are free; NRSV settings are not).

### Ash Wednesday & Triduum — model as OVERLAY/conditional insertions on the base Word+Meal ordo (~70% ordinary)
- **Ash Wednesday:** after sermon insert Litany of Penitence[LIC] + Imposition of Ashes[LIC,
  "Remember that you are dust…", repeated per person]; no Alleluia; muted close.
- **Maundy Thursday:** Word → Footwashing[LIC, opt] → full Meal → Stripping of the Altar (silence,
  no closing blessing/dismissal — ends wordlessly).
- **Good Friday:** replace Prayers with Bidding Prayer[LIC extended litany] + Solemn Reproaches[LIC,
  opt] + Veneration of the Cross[LIC]; NO Meal; ends in silence, no blessing.
- **Vigil of Easter:** four-part macro-container — Service of Light (new fire, Paschal candle,
  Exsultet) / Service of Readings (up to 12 OT readings, each + psalm/canticle + collect) /
  Service of Baptism (baptisms + renewal of covenant) / Service of Communion (Gloria/Alleluia
  return). *Needs section-container capability.*

## Architecture implications (the important part for our build)
1. **No new block TYPES needed.** All six services decompose into the existing
   proper_slot / reading_slot / hymn_slot / literal_text / dialogue / notation / rubric taxonomy
   from LWS-0b — a strong validation of that schema.
2. **Two new STRUCTURAL capabilities required:**
   - **Linked / child rites** — Committal is a separate rite from the Funeral; a Funeral may embed
     the Meal. Need a rite that can reference/produce a companion rite document.
   - **Section containers** — the Vigil's four sub-services and its up-to-12-reading block need a
     grouping container above the block level.
3. **Triggering:** occasion rites (wedding/funeral) are event-triggered (no calendar date);
   Ash Wednesday/Triduum are date-triggered overlays on the base ordo (fits the Condition system).
4. **Entitlement:** every rite = a small permanent [PD] scaffold (bundle now) + [LIC] AF wording
   (gate on the church's S&S/AF license, same mechanism as Sunday) + [VAR] slots (existing
   machinery). No new entitlement design — reuse the Sunday model.

## Suggested build sequence (mine)
- **Now-tractable, no schema extension:** Service of the Word (derive from Holy Communion),
  the three Daily Offices (mostly [PD]). Author as DRAFT rites → owner verifies against ELW →
  ship. These prove occasion-rite authoring end to end.
- **Needs a schema extension first (linked/child rites + section containers):** Funeral+Committal,
  Vigil of Easter. Brief that extension before authoring them.
- **Single-rite but [LIC]-heavy:** Marriage — authorable without extension, but its value depends
  on the church's AF entitlement resolving the vows/pronouncement text.
- **Overlays:** Ash Wednesday, Maundy Thursday, Good Friday as conditional insertion sets on the
  base ordo (Condition system already supports this).

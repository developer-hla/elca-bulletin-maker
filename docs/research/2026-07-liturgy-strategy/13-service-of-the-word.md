# A Well-Planned ELCA Service of the Word (research)

July 22, 2026 · Research for Ascension's own Sunday use (lay-preacher-led, no communion)
and to correct our `elw_service_of_the_word` DRAFT rite. Sources: the authoritative ELW
**Service of the Word** on Sundays & Seasons (atom `elw_sow`), the **ELCA official supplemental
"Thanksgiving for the Word"** materials, and two synod-published lay-usable orders (South-Central
Synod of Wisconsin 2023, Gulf Coast Synod 2020). Structure/incipits only below — copyrighted ELW
prose is not reproduced; it resolves per-church via S&S entitlement.

## The headline: it is NOT "Communion minus the Meal"

A well-planned Service of the Word has a real **Thanksgiving** section where the Meal would be —
it does not just skip from the offering to the Lord's Prayer. The central element is a
**THANKSGIVING FOR THE WORD**: a prayer/litany of thanks *for the Word* that stands parallel to
the Great Thanksgiving over the meal. Confirmed identically across `elw_sow`, the ELCA supplemental,
and both synod orders.

**Two different "baptism/thanksgiving" things, often confused (this matters):**
- **Thanksgiving for the Word** — the *response* in place of communion (Thanksgiving section).
- **Thanksgiving for Baptism** — an alternative *opening* rite at the **Gathering**, used *in place
  of Confession and Forgiveness* (Gulf Coast: "The service may begin with confession and forgiveness
  (ELW p. 211) **or with thanksgiving for baptism (ELW p. 97)**"). This is likely what Ascension has
  reached for ad hoc — but its authoritative home is the Gathering (replacing Confession), not the
  Meal slot.

## The well-planned ordo (authoritative, lay-usable)

**GATHERING**
- Confession and Forgiveness **OR** Thanksgiving for Baptism (choose one)
- Gathering Hymn · Greeting · (Kyrie / Hymn of Praise — optional) · Prayer of the Day

**WORD**
- First Reading · Psalm · Second Reading · Gospel Acclamation · Gospel
- Sermon · Hymn of the Day · Creed (Apostles' or Nicene) · Prayers of Intercession · Peace

**THANKSGIVING** ← the response in place of the Meal (this is the piece our draft is missing)
- Offering + Offertory (a **Canticle of Thanksgiving** — ELW's are "Let the Vineyards Be Fruitful"
  ELW 181–184, or "Create in Me a Clean Heart" ELW 185–188; the ELCA supplement also gives
  "Salvation belongs to our God")
- **Thanksgiving for the Word** (the central prayer; the ELCA supplement notes it is "appropriately
  led from the **ambo**" — the reading desk, fitting for a lay preacher, not the altar)
- Lord's Prayer (with an intro such as "Gathered into one by the Holy Spirit")

**SENDING**
- Blessing · Sending Hymn · Dismissal

## Lay-preacher (no ordained presider) adaptations — grounded in the sources
- **Confession's declaration of forgiveness:** the SotW/synod forms use a *proclamation* form a
  layperson may lead — e.g. "In the name of Christ, your sins are forgiven" (SCSW, spoken by the
  leader) — rather than the pastoral "I declare to you…" absolution. Choosing **Thanksgiving for
  Baptism** at the Gathering sidesteps the absolution question entirely and is a clean lay option.
- **Blessing:** the Aaronic "The Lord bless you and keep you…" led by the leader is used (SCSW).
- **Thanksgiving for the Word** is led "from the ambo" — i.e. by the one who has led the Word,
  appropriate for a lay preacher.
- Synod templates that say "presiding minister" are the ELCA book's default wording; the lay-usable
  synod adaptations (SCSW uses "L:") show the layperson-led forms.

## What this means for our `elw_service_of_the_word` DRAFT rite — a real gap
Our current draft (block order: …prayers_of_intercession → peace → offering → lords_prayer →
blessing…) has NO **Canticle of Thanksgiving** and NO **Thanksgiving for the Word**. It is a
truncated Holy Communion, not a complete Service of the Word — exactly the "feels incomplete /
last-minute" problem. To make it well-planned:
1. Add a **Thanksgiving for the Word** element (proper_slot / canonical-style, ELW-text-via-entitlement
   + a PD/own fallback) between the offering and the Lord's Prayer.
2. Add an optional **Canticle of Thanksgiving** (offertory) in that section.
3. Make **Thanksgiving for Baptism** an alternative to Confession at the Gathering (a toggle/module),
   distinct from the mid-service baptism module we already have.
4. Confirm the Confession's forgiveness + the Blessing use lay-appropriate (proclamation/plural) forms
   for Ascension's lay-led use.
